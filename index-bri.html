About Bri

Psychology of Stories set in beautiful places, that are just a little wrong.

She studied writing and cinematography, moving to Los Angeles with wide eyes, and walked away from high pressure corporate sales to come back to what always haunted her, and the point where the fog meets the fracture.

Bri writes atmospheric horror that lingers like the damp cold of a morning mist.
